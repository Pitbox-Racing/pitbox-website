// 01 — About

What is
Pitbox?

Pitbox is a real-time sim racing companion for iPhone. It reads the live telemetry your sim broadcasts over Wi-Fi and turns it into a dashboard — the kind of information a race engineer would give you through a headset, now on your phone.

Your sim shows you what's happening on screen. Pitbox shows you what's happening to your car — sector deltas, tyre state, fuel load, gaps, penalties — presented clearly, without burying the signal in noise.

No cables. No extra hardware. Just your iPhone on your rig, connected over your local network.

Pitbox Racing Ltd is registered in England & Wales and headquartered in London. The app is in active development ahead of an iOS launch in 2026.

// 03 — Features

Data that
matters.

F — 01

Sector Deltas

Know exactly where you're gaining and losing time — sector by sector, against your personal best and your rival.

F — 02

Live Dashboard

A landscape display on iPhone showing real-time session data as you drive. Designed to be read at a glance, not studied.

F — 03

Session Modes

Dedicated layouts for Time Trial, Race, and Practice. The right data for the right context — nothing else.

F — 04

Car State

Tyre wear, fuel load, ERS, and damage — surfaced clearly, so you can make decisions your sim buries in menus.

F — 05

Race Situation

Gaps ahead and behind, penalties, lap count — the full picture of where you stand in the race.

F — 06

iPhone Native

Built entirely in Swift for iOS. Dark mode, landscape, high-frequency rendering. No subscriptions to get started.
